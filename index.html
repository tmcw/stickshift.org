Database exploration & analysis, the fast and simple way

Demo Source Code

Works with your database

Out of the box, Stickshift supports Postgres, Redshift, and MySQL. Compatibility with other database backends is incredibly easy to add.

Built-in Visualization

Line charts, stacked bars, grouped bars, and maps as result visualizations - without writing a line of code. Charts are responsive and can be exported with one click.

Save & Share Queries

Stickshift can save SQL queries as files on GitHub, transparently and simply. People can create complex queries, save them for later, and share them with permalinks.
